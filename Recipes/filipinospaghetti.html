What's Cooking

Filipino Spaghetti may not be a traditional filipino dish or your traditional spaghetti. But its roots trace all the way back to the 1940s. Many generations of Filipinos see the dish as a party staple and the taste is nothing like any tradtional Spaghetti. Let's take on this sweet spin, that is Filipino Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • -Box of Spaghetti
  • -Ground Beef or Vegan Crumbles
  • -3 hot dogs
  • -Jar of spaghetti sauce
  • -8oz pineapple juice
  • -1/2 to 1 cup sugar
  • salt
