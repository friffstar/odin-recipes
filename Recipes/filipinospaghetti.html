What's Cooking

Filipino Spaghetti may not be a traditional filipino dish or your traditional spaghetti. But its roots trace all the way back to the 1940s. Many generations of Filipinos see the dish as a party staple and the taste is nothing like any tradtional Spaghetti. Let's take on this sweet spin, that is Filipino Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • -Box of Spaghetti
  • -Ground Beef or Vegan Crumbles
  • -3 hot dogs
  • -Jar of spaghetti sauce
  • -8oz pineapple juice
  • -1/2 to 1 cup sugar
  • salt

Let's Cook!

  1. Fill a pot with water and bring to a boil
  2. While we wait for the water, take a pan add a 2 tablesppons of oil and bring it up to medium heat
  3. Add ground beef or vegan crumbles to pan, stirring and breaking the meat up as small as you can. Toss around occassionally to prevent burning
  4. Water should be boiling. Add a iberal amount of salt to the water and toss in the pasta. Stir occassionally to prevent sticking
  5. Take the hot dogs and slice into 1/4 inch rounds and toss into the pan with the meat. Mox to incorporate the the meat and hotdogs
  6. After cooking meat for another 2 mins add the jar of sauce and 6oz of pineapple juice, along with the sugar. Add enough sugar to make it sweet. Bring heat down to a medium-low and cook for another 5 mins
  7. Pasta should be done and al-dente. Turn off the heat, drain the water and place the pasta back into the pot
  8. Turn heat off for the meat sauce and add to pot of pasta. Mix until sauce is well incorporated with pasta

COOKED!
